# Industry Calibration Guide

Adapt tone, vocabulary, and emphasis based on the target company's industry. When a company doesn't fit a known category, research it and calibrate dynamically.

## Fintech / Financial Services
**Companies:** Stripe, Datavant, State Street
**Tone:** Precise, data-driven, impact-first
**Lead with:** Quantified metrics, financial systems, compliance, data pipelines
**Key ATS terms:** SQL, Agile, KPIs, stakeholder alignment, regulatory compliance, data integrity, API, Snowflake, risk management
**Culture signals (Stripe):** "users first," high autonomy, rigorous thinking, real-world impact, writing culture
**Culture signals (State Street):** institutional, risk-aware, regulatory, process-driven, global scale
**Culture signals (Datavant):** mission-driven, health data, privacy-first, interoperability, de-identification

## Legal / Compliance / GRC
**Companies:** Law Society, Diligent
**Tone:** Formal, governance-aware, risk-conscious
**Lead with:** Regulatory frameworks, audit trails, process documentation, policy
**Key ATS terms:** GRC, GDPR, compliance, risk management, requirements analysis, governance, audit, documentation, stakeholder management
**Culture signals (Law Society):** public interest, professional standards, formal communication, member-focused
**Culture signals (Diligent):** modern governance, SaaS, board-level stakeholders, ESG, transparency

## Energy / Sustainability
**Companies:** YunoEnergy
**Tone:** Operational, outcome-focused, mission-aware
**Lead with:** Process optimisation, cross-functional delivery, operational reporting
**Key ATS terms:** Operational efficiency, data analysis, reporting, process improvement, energy systems, sustainability metrics
**Culture signals:** Green transition, customer impact, operational excellence, data-driven decisions

## Engineering Consulting
**Companies:** Expleo, WhiteTech
**Tone:** Versatile, client-delivery focused, adaptable
**Lead with:** Client outcomes, stakeholder management, delivery track record
**Key ATS terms:** Business analysis, requirements gathering, delivery, client management, process mapping, SDLC, UAT
**Culture signals (Expleo):** quality assurance, engineering excellence, multi-sector, global delivery
**Culture signals (WhiteTech):** tech services, agile delivery, client-embedded teams

## Manufacturing / Operations
**Companies:** Kepak
**Tone:** Practical, efficiency-driven, results-focused
**Lead with:** Process improvement, supply chain, operational metrics, cost reduction
**Key ATS terms:** ERP, process mapping, lean, continuous improvement, supply chain, operational efficiency, data analysis
**Culture signals:** Safety-first, operational discipline, continuous improvement, team-oriented

## Healthcare / Healthtech
**Tone:** Mission-driven, compliance-aware, patient-outcome focused
**Lead with:** Data integrity, regulatory compliance, interoperability, patient outcomes
**Key ATS terms:** HIPAA, HL7, FHIR, data governance, clinical workflows, EHR, compliance
**Culture signals:** Patient-first, evidence-based, privacy, trust

## SaaS / Product Companies
**Tone:** User-focused, metrics-driven, iterative
**Lead with:** Product metrics, user outcomes, A/B testing, sprint delivery
**Key ATS terms:** Product analytics, user stories, sprint planning, OKRs, feature delivery, stakeholder alignment
**Culture signals:** Ship fast, iterate, data-informed decisions, customer obsession

## Dynamic Calibration
For companies not in the above list:
1. Identify their industry from the JD and company website
2. Research their careers page for culture language
3. Check Glassdoor for employee sentiment
4. Map to the closest industry template above
5. Override specific terms with company-specific language from the JD
